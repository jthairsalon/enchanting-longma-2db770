JT Hair Salon has served the Torrance community with dedication, craft, and warmth. Our spacious studio is designed to put you at ease the moment you walk through the door — plants, natural light, and attentive stylists who listen.

Whether it's a classic cut, a bold color transformation, or a relaxing treatment, we bring skill and care to every chair. Our team of experienced stylists is committed to making you look and feel your absolute best.
